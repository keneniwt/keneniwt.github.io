Welcome to my page! I am a third-year PhD student at Swansea University, specializing in 3D computer vision. I received my MSc in Computer Science from Swansea University in September 2022, and my BEng in Electrical Engineering and Automation from China Three Gorges University in July 2021. My current research focuses on point cloud completion, with broader interests in 3D vision and geometric deep learning.
